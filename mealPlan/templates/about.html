This is our CI-102-103 Project! 

 Our Vision :

Our product concept is a website that allows a user to organize and set up meal plans based on the ingredients that they choose. If the user does not have a certain ingredient, the  website can also redirect them to websites or stores that sell this ingredient at the best price. Also, depending on whether the user wants to lose weight, gain muscle, or whatever  their goals are that they set for themselves, they can enter in this data as well and the website will output meal plans that accommodate to the user’s desires. We will essentially build  a platform that allows users to accomplish their goals with healthy and affordable eating habits.

 Our Mission:

Build a platform that allows users to accomplish their goals by creating an efficient way to display healthy and affordable meals.
